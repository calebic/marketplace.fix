Update 5 Hotfix 1 - Internet Lookup Timeout

Changes:
- manual Internet Lookup window now uses a larger dedicated timeout budget than the general lookup timeout used by background workflows
- manual lookup preserves partial candidate results when cancellation happens after some candidates have already been found
- trusted-source manual lookup now exits earlier once a strong priced candidate has already been found

Goal:
- reduce cases where the Internet Lookup window reports a timeout before returning any result
- prefer returning decent partial/manual candidates instead of discarding them when the lookup budget expires late in the process
