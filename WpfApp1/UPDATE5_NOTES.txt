Update 5: inference + lookup accuracy

Changes in this handoff:
- Added explicit confidence/evidence fields to VehicleConfigItem:
  - ConfidenceScore
  - ConfidenceTier
  - IdentityEvidence
  - ValuationEvidence
- Applied those fields during autofill and review-hold flows so config state now carries:
  - why the identity was chosen
  - how strong it was
  - what valuation evidence was used
- Strengthened review-hold gating so weak identity/valuation combinations are more likely to hold for review instead of silently applying.
- Tightened identity conflict handling in VehicleInferenceService:
  - stronger local mod-level evidence can now resist weaker conflicting online identity suggestions
  - conflicting strong online vs local signals are more likely to be marked suspicious instead of blindly overriding
- Separated valuation adoption from identity confidence more cleanly:
  - online pricing is less willing to auto-apply when identity is still weak/suspicious unless the pricing confidence is very strong
- Reduced negative lookup cache TTL from 6 hours to 1 hour so bad/weak misses do not stick around as long.
- Improved manual internet lookup result ordering:
  - verified/likely sources rank better
  - make/model/year/body-style matches are weighted more explicitly
  - very weak candidates are filtered out
- Improved lookup evidence text to include match-quality style wording and clearer recommendation context.

Intended user-visible effects:
- fewer weak/conflicting autofill guesses getting silently applied
- more items held for review when identity is questionable
- more understandable progress/detail text during autofill
- manual lookup results should feel more relevant and less random
